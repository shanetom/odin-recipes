Navajo Fry Bread

As far as I know this is the original recipe for Navajo Bread. It's is great with honey and butter. The Navajo put chiles, cheese, onion, lettuce and beans on it, and call them Nava Tacos.

  • 2 cups oil for frying
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 2 ½ cups warm milk
  1. In a large heavy bottomed frying pan, heat 1 inch of vegetable oil or lard to 365 degrees F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt and milk; mix well.
  3. Place 3 or 4 at a time into the hot oil.
