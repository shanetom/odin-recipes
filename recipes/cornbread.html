Cornbread

Fluffy and delicious cornbread that goes great with any dish!

  • 1 (15.25 ounce) can sweet corn
  • 1 cup cornbread mix
  • 1 cup pancake mix
  • ½ cup evaporated milk
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Combine corn, cornbread mix, pancake mix, evaporated milk, sugar, oil, butter, honey, and salt in a large bowl; whisk together.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until golden and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 30 minutes.
